About

Hello, I’m Omkar Kakade, a software engineer and blogger with a strong interest in technology, programming, and explaining what I learn as I go.

My main interests are high-performance computing, C++, CUDA, GPU programming, open source work, and machine learning. Most of what I write here grows out of that overlap between building software, performance-focused engineering, and careful technical documentation.

The goal of this site is simple: explore, learn, and contribute to the world of technology while sharing that process with people who care about the craft. You can reach me through GitHub, LinkedIn, or X.
