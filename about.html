About

I am a programmer who likes small systems, readable interfaces, and notes that stay useful after the day they were written.

Most of my work sits somewhere between building software, debugging ideas, and trying to explain technical decisions in plain language. I keep this site intentionally spare so the writing carries the weight, not the chrome around it.

If a project teaches me something, it usually ends up here: a short post, a tracker update, or a link in the projects list with enough context to remember why it mattered.
